# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

kvault-mcp is an engineering project. We expect contributors and maintainers to:

- Focus feedback on the code, the architecture, and the evidence — not on the person.
- Show up with working reproductions, not vibes.
- Acknowledge disagreement about technical direction and resolve it through ADRs, benchmarks, or reversible experiments.
- Treat people with respect regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or sustained disruption in issues, PRs, or discussions.
- Sexualized content, imagery, or advances.
- Publishing others' private information without their permission.
- Deliberately derailing technical decisions with non-technical arguments.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to the maintainer (see `README.md` author section or repository contact). Reports will be reviewed within 7 days. Possible responses:

- Private warning with an explanation of what was unacceptable.
- Public correction in the relevant issue or PR.
- Temporary or permanent ban from contributing.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) and tailored for a small technical project. It intentionally avoids rules that are not enforceable in practice.
